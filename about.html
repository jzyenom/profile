Who We Are

Zinta Africa: A Catalyst for Change Across the Continent

Zinta Africa is a transformative institution that integrates education, humanitarian service, and community development to uplift individuals and communities across Africa. Established in The Gambia, we operate as a company, NGO, and academic institution to drive meaningful impact through innovative programs and sustainable initiatives.


Our work spans four African countries, addressing critical challenges such as education inequality, poverty alleviation, and youth and women empowerment. By combining hands-on action with a commitment to excellence, Zinta Africa is shaping a brighter future for Africa, one life at a time.


Our Mission, Vision, and Values
  • Mission

    • To empower individuals through education, foster community resilience through humanitarian efforts, and create opportunities for sustainable development across Africa.

  • Vision

    • To be a leading institution driving social transformation, equality, and prosperity in Africa, ensuring every individual has the tools to thrive.

  • Core Values
    • o Integrity: We operate with transparency and uphold the highest ethical standards.
    • o Empowerment: We believe in unlocking the potential within every individual and community.
    • o Sustainability: Our programs are designed to create lasting, positive change.
    • o Collaboration: Working together with stakeholders, partners, and communities is at the heart of our approach.
    • o Innovation: We embrace creative solutions to address complex challenges.
