Featured Programs and Initiatives

Education for All Initiative

Offering a wide range of academic programs, certifications, and professional development courses to empower the next generation of leaders.

Humanitarian Relief Projects

Delivering aid and implementing community-focused projects in healthcare, food security, and education across four African countries.

Capacity Building

Empowering individuals and communities with the tools and knowledge to thrive in a global economy.

Innovation and Research

Driving impactful change through interdisciplinary research and innovation.

Women and Youth Empowerment Program

Focused on skill-building workshops, mentorship opportunities, and small business funding to empower women and young entrepreneurs.

Health and Well-Being Campaign

About

Zinta Africa is a transformative institution that integrates education, humanitarian service, and community development to uplift individuals and communities across Africa. Established in The Gambia, we operate as a company, NGO, and academic institution to drive meaningful impact through innovative programs and sustainable initiatives.
