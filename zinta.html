About Zinta Institute

Zinta is a private institution of higher education with an interdisciplinary approach, founded with a transformative vision: to be a global educational hub providing hybrid and autodidactic learning styles. The institution focuses on raising global leaders who are prepared to change their world with purposeful mindsets and strong character, guided by integrity.

Zinta equips students to be agents of renewal, stepping into the public square to bring positive transformation for the glory of God. We believe in the uniqueness of every individual, helping them uncover their calling and maximize their potential through academic training, leadership development, and skills enhancement.

Our programs are designed to impact communities, foster research, and create positive societal change. Zinta offers affordable and contextual academic training at Levels 4, 5, 6, 7, and 8, with diplomas affiliated with reputable institutions in Africa and the United States. We partner with organizations to deliver capacity building, leadership training, environmental sustainability, project management, healthcare, consultancy, and agricultural initiatives.

Mission

Zinta Institute is dedicated to serving the African people and the global community through academic quality, inclusive achievement, and community service. We aim to:

  • 1. Foster sustainability, critical thinking, creativity, and student achievement.
  • 2. Promote justice, equity, and respect for the dignity and rights of others.

    • Our success lies in aligning our programs with the demands of the modern world, providing senior managers with the strategic vision and skills necessary to address global challenges across public and private sectors.


    Key Objectives:
    • Promote peace-building through tolerance and respect for human dignity.
    • Develop poverty reduction interventions for sustainable development.
    • Train and certify individuals in contextualized fields of study relevant to Africa's growth.
Vision

To be a sustainable leader in innovative education, combining knowledge, skills, and life-changing experiences to transform the mind, body, and spirit.


Core Values
  • Competence: Striving for excellence in education and professional development.
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and responsibility in all actions.
  • Leadership: Nurturing global leaders with the vision to inspire change.

    • University Motto: Dream it, Believe it, Achieve it!
